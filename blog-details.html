How to build momentum in online learning

Small rituals, clear goals, and steady review make a huge difference in long-term retention.

The key to online learning is not motivation alone. It is structure, feedback, and consistent practice. Start with a weekly target, review notes at the same time each day, and pair each lesson with one small project.

When you connect learning to action, the experience becomes memorable and practical. That is what turns passive watching into lasting skill-building.
